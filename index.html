Join us at Flex Club Kapza

Events

At Flex Club Kapza, we offer an array of invigorating events, including powerlifting, crossfit, and cardio-focused activities such as group runs. Our community comprises individuals who share a passion for training and seek a sense of belonging within a supportive community. Regardless of your age or fitness level, we welcome everyone to participate in our events without any apprehensions about not being "good enough" or "fit enough." These events occur frequently throughout the year, and you may always check our calendar to find one that piques your interest. Moreover, our exceptional trainers are always available to assist you in attaining your fitness goals and will accompany you during the event. We are dedicated to helping you get into shape and fostering a friendly environment for all.

Powerlifting Events

Powerlifting: Our powerlifting events cater to individuals who want to focus on building strength, endurance, and muscle mass. Participants use a barbell and perform three exercises: squat, bench press, and deadlift. Our expert trainers are always on hand to provide guidance on technique and safety.

Crossfit Events

Crossfit: Crossfit is a high-intensity, functional fitness program that combines elements of cardio, weightlifting, and gymnastics. Our Crossfit events are designed to challenge participants both physically and mentally, with workouts that vary in duration and intensity. Our trainers can modify the exercises to suit individual fitness levels, ensuring that everyone gets a great workout.

Cardio Focused Events

Introducing our Cardio Fusion event, a high-intensity, functional fitness program that combines elements of cardio, strength training, and agility. Our Cardio Fusion events are designed to challenge participants both physically and mentally, with workouts that vary in duration and intensity. Our expert trainers can modify the exercises to suit individual fitness levels, ensuring that everyone gets a great workout.

A little more about us!

Welcome to the website of Luka and Lena Bjekovic, the founders of a fitness gym that is dedicated to helping people of all ages and fitness levels achieve their goals. Our gym is designed to offer a unique fitness experience that combines the passion and expertise of both Luka and Lena. Luka is a fitness expert with a focus on bodybuilding and powerlifting. He has helped numerous clients achieve their fitness goals through customized workout plans and proper lifting techniques. Luka is committed to creating a supportive environment where clients can push themselves to their limits and achieve their desired results. Lena is an experienced CrossFit and cardio coach who has a passion for functional movements and high-intensity workouts. She is dedicated to helping clients improve their endurance and overall fitness levels through challenging yet supportive training sessions. Together, Luka and Lena have created a gym that caters to the needs of all fitness enthusiasts. Whether you are a seasoned athlete or just starting out on your fitness journey, our gym provides a welcoming environment that will help you reach your goals. At our gym, we believe that fitness is not just about looking good, but also about feeling good and living a healthy lifestyle. We are committed to helping our clients achieve their fitness goals through our expertise, passion, and dedication. Thank you for visiting our website, and we look forward to welcoming you to our gym soon.
